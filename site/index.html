02 Three-concern separation

Three authorities.
One defensible boundary.

I

Scientific support

What the verified formulation, implementation, data, experiment, or cited literature actually establishes.

II

Method reporting

What the applicable optimization, ML, simulation, systems, or engineering method requires authors to disclose.

III

Venue compliance

What the target publication’s current official instructions and template require—kept separate from scientific truth.
